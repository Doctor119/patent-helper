What is a preliminary search?

A preliminary search is one to simply get started on your journey to obtaining a patent. Obtaining a patent is a process that takes many hours of research, several drafts of a patent application, and possibly many years in the patent prosecution process. Before you get started on such a heavy commitment, we recommend you do a preliminary search of the patent database to get a quick idea of how novel your invention is.

If at the end of this preliminary search you feel hopeful about obtaining a patent for your invention, you should consider calling the experts at Lawyer, Lawyer and Associates to begin this exciting and prestigious process.
